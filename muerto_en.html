About game

The project was initially created for classes in 2D Graphics, the goals were to show the Mexican holiday of DÃ­a de Muertos (Day of the Dead), which was presented in Story mode. In addition, I did the Arena mode, which was based on Mobs Inc, where player have to survive 14 waves of enemies, fight 4 types of enemies and finally face the boss. Player play as Luchador, who has a basic attack and two special attacks that can be unlocked after reaching the appropriate character level. After defeating the boss, an additional Luchador skin is unlocked, which can be used in this mode.
